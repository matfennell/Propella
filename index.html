Welcome to SPIAGGIA (beach): a BEACH inspired concept offering a taste of ITALIAN BEACH FOOD in the heart of FULHAM.

Our all day menu allows for a laid back yet bespoke dining experience where customers can settle for a few light PIATTINI (small plates), a fantastically fresh SALAD, or push the boat out with a selection of our signature CHARGRILLED SPIEDINI (skewers).

We also offer DAILY SPECIALS, delicious PASTA and the best PIADINA (quintessential Italian beach staple) this side of Italy.

Add to that a selection of classic beach COCKTAILS, a harmoniously assembled WINE LIST (all available by the glass), a UNIQUE setting, a predilection for FUN, and you're nearly there...

It's always a good time to hit the Spiaggia, where summer never end. RILLASARSI (relax) and TUFFARSI (dive in)!
